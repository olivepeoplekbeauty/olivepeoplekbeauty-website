OUR STORY

Beauty Beyond Borders

Where Heritage Meets Innovation

OLIVE PEOPLE was created with one simple vision: bringing together the rich traditions of Jordan and the world-leading innovation of Korean Beauty.

Jordan has been home to olive trees for thousands of years. Their symbolism of peace, life and wellness perfectly reflects the values we believe every customer deserves.

Korea has become a global leader in skincare, cosmetics and beauty technology through continuous research and uncompromising quality.

OLIVE PEOPLE combines these two cultures into one destination where customers can experience premium products in a welcoming environment.
