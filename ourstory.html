OUR STORY

Beauty rooted
in connection.

Born between Seoul and Amman, OLIVE PEOPLE brings together Korean innovation and the timeless spirit of the olive tree.

WHY WE BEGAN

More than
a beauty store.

We imagined a place where beauty could be discovered slowly, honestly and with confidence.

OLIVE PEOPLE began with a simple belief: exceptional products become meaningful when people can experience them for themselves. We connect carefully selected Korean beauty, wellness and lifestyle brands with the people of Jordan through a warm, welcoming retail experience.

Every product on our shelves is chosen with purpose. Every detail in our space is designed to make discovery feel natural, personal and inspiring.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Modern beauty, thoughtful selection and genuine human connection — all under one roof.

TWO PLACES, ONE VISION

From Seoul
to Amman.

Seoul
